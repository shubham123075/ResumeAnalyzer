About the platform

Designed for students, job seekers, and professionals.

AI Resume Reviewer helps candidates understand how their resume may appear to automated screening tools and recruiters. It focuses on clarity, measurable strengths, technical skill coverage, and practical edits that can improve the quality of an application.

Instead of giving vague feedback, the review separates your result into summary, skills, strengths, weaknesses, suggestions, and an ATS score so you can improve the document section by section.
